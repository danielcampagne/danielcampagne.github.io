An opportunity for young chefs to show their worth

CANN is an proposal supported by the bottled water company Aguas de Mondariz. It's a contest that groups the best young spanish chefs to compete.

The website provides a simple interface intended to clearly call for a participation. After a short introduction, the user gets to the inscription form. The look and feel aims to be simple and elegant but warm and near.
