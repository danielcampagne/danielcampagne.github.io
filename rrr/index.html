RIDE & REVIVE GIRONA

September 29th - October 3rd, 2018 5 days of cycling and yoga in stunning Catalonia
Northern Catalonia’s largest city, Girona is a less known jewel with its web of cobbled lanes, medieval walls and catalan charm. In the past years Girona has become a training destination and home of many worlds best pro cyclist. Lets discover the foothills of the Pyrenees and the stretches towards the beaches of the Costa Brava on the bike. After the rides the daily yoga with its long stretches and calming centering breaths will help our bodies and minds revive. A team of former pro cyclists will guide our training on the road and Steph Cusack will lead the Yin Yoga sessions. We will sample local cuisine, and of course wine, discover tranquil villages and scenery and lively meditarenean evenings. Join us for this late summer adventure!
OUR HOME IN THE COUNTRYSIDE OF GIRONA

This retreat is for men and women, who want an active adventurous break from their daily life. Our home and backdrop for this retreat will be the beautiful city and its countryside of Girona. EXPERIENCE LEVELS

There will be two groups and experiences levels for our training on the road. Your level will be determined by a FTP test and an evaluation at the beginning of our stay.

Group 1 - designed for experienced cyclists who want to challenge themself with an average of 90km and 1200m altitude per day
Group 2 - designed to suit rides who are relatively new to cycling and want. This group will take on an average of 50km per day with a slightly less challenging altitude approach.
Yin Yoga is suitable for all levels. Mats, bolsters and blocks are provided.
