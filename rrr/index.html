RIDE & REVIVE GIRONA

September 29th - October 3rd, 2018 5 days of cycling and yoga in stunning Catalonia
Northern Catalonia’s largest city, Girona is a lesser known jewel with its maze of cobble stoned lanes, medieval walls and Catalan charm. In recent years Girona has become a training destination and home of many of the world’s best professional cyclists. We will explore the foothills of the Pyrenees and its winding landscape occasionally venturing up into the peaks themself. After the daily rides a relaxing session of yoga with its deept stretches and breathing will help our bodies and minds revive. A team of former pro cyclists will guide our training on the road and Steph Cusack will lead the Yin Yoga sessions. We will feast on local cuisine and wine, discover tranquil villages and scenery and enjoy lively mediterranean evenings. Join us for this late summer adventure!
OUR HOME IN THE COUNTRYSIDE OF GIRONA

WHO IS IT FOR

This adventure is for men and women, who want an active, invigorating break from their daily lives. Our home and backdrop will be the beautiful city of Girona and its surrounding countryside. During our daily cycling we will indulge our wanderlust, explore the Pyrenees and beautiful Catalonia. These rides will be tailored to our guests’ interests and fitness level, so whether you are a beginner or an experienced cyclist we will make sure to accomodate your needs. The Yin Yoga session in the evening will provide a welcome balance to the days cycling and your daily life.

EXPERIENCE LEVELS

There will be two groups and experience levels for our training on the road. Your level will be determined by a FTP test and an evaluation at the beginning of our stay.

Group 1 - designed for experienced cyclists who want to challenge themself with an average of 90km and 1200m altitude per day.
Group 2 - designed to suit rides who are relatively new to cycling and want to explore it further. This group will take on an average of 50km per day with a slightly less challenging altitude approach.

Yin Yoga is suitable for both levels. Mats, bolsters and blocks will be provided.
