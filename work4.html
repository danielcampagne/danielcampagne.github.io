An underground tour agency

Urban Culture Tours in an enterprise dedicated to do underground tours around Berlin. It focuses on a young target audience, mostly tourists, erasmus students and local students.

Every tour profile includes a stop-motion video, parts of the route that provides the user with an easy way to understand what every tour contains while making it more desirable. The lower section of the page is designed to provide detailed information about the tour.
