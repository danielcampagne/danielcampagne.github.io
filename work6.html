Second work: Kutter for Spotify

Kutter is a concept of mixtable made for work on Spotify. The app would have two sides, one for mixing and other one for follow a dj who is mixing. Resuming, you play and your friends listen.

It’s the minimum expression of a mixtable, one that would fit for casual djs. And it would have some extra tools when you hover the wave for make and resize loops.

Anyway the Spotify API has some limitations that would make impossible to make a mixtable, but it was a funny thing to develop.

This one were made with Photoshop.
